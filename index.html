About Rythm Rider


Rhythm Rider is a E-Dancing show which is being organized by Student organization Factum & Eminent on the National level.

We are giving a chance to the talent to compete across the nation and win great prizes.

It is divided in 3 categories Solo, duo & Crew

It is the platform on which anyone can participate. We believe that talent cannot be measured by the age/language/region etc.

Winning criteria

1

Auditions

Registration and submit the recorded video for audition. Video will be uploaded on our social media and top 70% will be selected, Out of 70% top 50% for Round-1 and rest 20% for wildcart entry on the basis of online voting on your audition video.

2

Round-1

These 50% will compete for round-2 and top 20% will be selected for the round-2 by our judges.

3

Wild card

In this 1 best will get chance to directly perform in round-2

4

Round-2

These 20% and 2-4% of wildcard participants will perform in this round and top 8% will be selected for finally by judges .

5

Final

Top 8% will perform in the finals and winner will be selected by judges.
